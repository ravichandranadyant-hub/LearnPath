Our Mission

The problem with education — and how we're fixing it

Millions of students have the ability but not the access to organised, free learning materials. LearnPath exists to close that gap — without charging anyone, ever.

⚠ Problems we identified
💸
Quality education is expensive
Top coaching centres charge large fees most families cannot afford, creating an unequal playing field.
🔀
Random video discovery wastes hours
Searching "calculus" returns hundreds of videos with no clear order. Students don't know what to watch or when.
📝
Free notes online are too shallow
Most free notes are one-paragraph summaries — not enough to actually understand a topic or pass an exam.
❓
Practice tests are scarce or paywalled
Three-question quizzes don't prepare students. Proper tests require paid subscriptions.
👩‍🏫
Educators have no easy outlet
Many retired teachers and professionals want to contribute but have no organised platform.
✅ How LearnPath solves it
1
Videos in lesson order. Every topic's videos are numbered 1, 2, 3… Watch from top to bottom — no searching.
2
Detailed 2-page notes. Each topic has thorough written notes with formulas, worked examples, and tables — only here.
3
15+ question tests. Proper tests with difficulty ratings, instant explanations, and a score breakdown.
4
We link, we don't host. Videos live on YouTube/MIT/NPTEL. We link directly — no copyright issues.
5
Free, always. No ads, no subscriptions. Sustained by voluntary contributions and grants.
The resultA student anywhere gets the same organised, sequenced, deeply-noted, and properly-tested learning as someone at a top coaching centre — for free.

Common questions
Do I need to be a professional teacher?
No. We welcome anyone with genuine subject expertise — engineers, doctors, researchers, working professionals. We verify through a qualification review or a short subject test.
How much time does it take?
Writing one topic (2-page notes + 15 questions) takes roughly 4–6 hours. Curating a video playlist takes 2–3 hours. No minimum commitment — contribute as and when you can.
Who owns what I write?
You do. You grant LearnPath a non-exclusive Creative Commons licence. You can publish the same content elsewhere and can withdraw at any time with reasonable notice.
Can I contribute in Tamil, Hindi or another regional language?
Yes — and we particularly need regional language contributors. We support English, Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Bengali, Gujarati, Odia, and Urdu.

How LearnPath Works

A free learning navigator. We put 224 videos in curriculum order, write original 2-page notes, and provide proper 15-question tests. Videos open on their original platform. Notes and tests only live here.

The model — why no copyright issues

Linking to publicly available content is legal everywhere. We are a directory: we sequence what already exists freely, then add original notes and tests on top. We never embed, download, or reproduce any video.

Sources we link to

YouTube — Khan Academy, 3Blue1Brown, CrashCourse, The Organic Chemistry Tutor, Veritasium, Physics Wallah, freeCodeCamp, Professor Leonard, Corey Schafer
MIT OpenCourseWare — Full university lectures, open access, no login
NPTEL — IIT & IISc professors, excellent for Indian curriculum
CrashCourse — Animated YouTube explainers across all subjects
Ted-Ed — Short animated lessons on YouTube
